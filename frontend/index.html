Welcome to HerRaise Hub

HerRaise Hub is a safe, inclusive, and empowering digital space for girls and young women in South Sudan and beyond. We provide mentorship, career resources, real-life stories of impact, and an interactive space to connect, learn, and grow together.

Our Mission

To empower girls and young women through mentorship, storytelling, and access to life-changing educational resources that support personal and professional development.

Our Vision

We envision a continent where every girl rises beyond societal limitations to lead, influence, and thrive — becoming the author of her own story and a role model for others.

Our Core Values

  • Empowerment: We believe in uplifting every girl’s voice and potential.
  • Inclusivity: Every girl matters, no matter where she comes from.
  • Growth: We encourage continuous learning, mentorship, and bold dreams.
  • Community: We grow stronger together — through shared stories and support.
  • Integrity: We lead with honesty, respect, and purpose.
